Arryn

"As High as Honor." House Arryn of the Eyrie is one of the Great Houses of Westeros. It has ruled over the Vale of Arryn for millennia.

Baelish

House Baelish of Harrenhal is an extinct noble house that held fealty to House Arryn of the Vale. Petyr Baelish, called "Littlefinger", was the only living member of the house at the beginning of the fourth century.

Baratheon

"Ours Is the Fury." House Baratheon of Storm's End is a Great House of Westeros. House Baratheon became the royal house of the Seven Kingdoms after Robert Baratheon led a rebellion against the Targaryen dynasty.

Bolton

"Our Blades Are Sharp." House Bolton of the Dreadfort is an extinctÂ Great House from the North. House Bolton was infamous for its centuries-old practice of flaying their enemies alive, to the point that they used a flayed man as their House sigil.

Clegane

House Clegane of Clegane's Keep is an extinct vassal house that holds fealty to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. They are landed knights rather than a full noble house, but have strong ties to the Lannisters.

Forrester

"Iron From Ice." House Forrester of Ironrath is an exiled noble house from the Wolfswood in the North of Westeros. They are vassals of the Glovers of Deepwood Motte, as well as staunch Stark bannermen.

Glover

House Glover of Deepwood Motte is a noble house from the Wolfswood in the North of Westeros. They are loyal vassals of House Stark of Winterfell, and have their seat at the wooden stronghold Deepwood Motte.

Greyjoy

"What Is Dead May Never Die." House Greyjoy of Pyke is one of the Great Houses of Westeros. It rules over the Iron Islands, a harsh and bleak collection of islands off the west coast of Westeros, from the castle at Pyke.

Iron Throne

The Iron Throne is the throne upon which the King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men sits. The Iron Throne was forged at the order of Aegon the Conqueror, the first of the Targaryen Kings, who conquered six of the seven independent kingdoms of Westeros and unified them under his rule.

Lannister

"Hear me roar!" House Lannister of Casterly Rock is one of the Great Houses of Westeros, one of it's richest and most powerful families and oldest dynasties. Their seat is Casterly Rock, a massive rocky promontory overlooking the Sunset Sea which has had habitations and fortifications built into it over the millennia.

Reed

House Reed of Greywater Watch is a vassal house that holds fealty to House Stark of Winterfell. They control the vast area of swampland and bog stretching across the narrowest part of the continent of Westeros, the Neck.

Stark

"Winter is Coming." House Stark of Winterfell is a Great House of Westeros, ruling over the vast region known as the North from their seat in Winterfell. It is one of the oldest lines of Westerosi nobility by far, claiming a line of descent stretching back over eight thousand years.

Targaryen

"Fire and Blood." House Targaryen of King's Landing is a Great House of Westeros and the current ruling royal House of the Seven Kingdoms. House Targaryen conquered and unified the realm before it was deposed during Robert's Rebellion and House Baratheon replaced it as the new royal House.

Tully

"Family, Duty, Honor." House Tully of Riverrun is a deposed Great House of Westeros. At the onset of the War of the Five Kings, House Tully came under attack by House Lannister and lent its support for House Stark when they came to their aid in the liberation of the Riverlands.

Tyrell

"Growing Strong." House Tyrell of Highgarden is an extinct Great House of Westeros. It ruled over the Reach, a vast, fertile, and heavily-populated region of southwestern Westeros.
