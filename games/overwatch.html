Better Profile Pictures

Please use these instead of any default profile picture given to you by discord, on the house!
Just click on the icon you want to download, and then follow these steps:

  1. Go to user settings on the bottom left of Discord.
  2. Click the edit button next to you profile.
  3. Click on your profile picture to change avatar.
  4. Find where you saved your new icon.
  5. Click save and you're done!

Better Profile Pictures

Please use these instead of any default profile picture given to you by discord.
Just tap on the icon you want, and then follow these steps:

  1. Go to your discord server section of your device.
  2. Tap on the settings gear near the bottom.
  3. Go to My Account.
  4. Tap on your picture.
  5. Find your new icon.
  6. Tap the save button on the bottom right and you're done!
